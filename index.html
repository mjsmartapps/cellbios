About CellBios

CellBios Healthcare & Lifesciences Pvt. Ltd is a Chennai-based biotech company specializing in cryopreservation, bioprocessing, and medical consumables for advanced healthcare and research applications.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To improve human life by delivering innovative, reliable, and cost-effective biotechnology and healthcare solutions that meet global standards.

Vision: To become a globally recognized leader in biotechnology and medical device manufacturing, especially in cell & gene therapy and advanced bioprocessing solutions.

Core Areas of Expertise

  • Cryopreservation Technology
  • Bioprocessing Systems
  • Blood Banking Solutions
  • Cell & Gene Therapy (CGT)
  • Tissue and Organ Storage

Manufacturing Excellence

We operate advanced manufacturing facilities equipped with Class 10,000 cleanroom environments, sterile and non-sterile production units, and advanced sealing/polymer technology strictly following ISO 13485 standards.

Industries We Serve

Hospitals & Healthcare Centers • Biotechnology Companies • Pharmaceutical Industries • Research Labs • Blood & Tissue Banks
